## Project Overview

The website for Rabbi Gene Wernick serves as a digital home for his extensive body of work, including sermons, articles, and community initiatives. It was designed to reflect his warmth and intellectual depth while providing easy access to his teachings for a global audience.

## My Role

I was responsible for the end-to-end design and development of the website. This included content architecture, visual design, and implementing a robust content management system to allow for frequent updates to his blog and sermon archive.

## How It Works

The site features a searchable database of sermons, a blog for long-form articles, and an integrated contact system for community engagement. The layout is optimized for readability across all devices, ensuring that his message is accessible whether viewed on a desktop or a smartphone.

THe biggest challange was creating a website that could effectively showcase Rabbi Wernick's extensive body of work and career experience, while also being user-friendly.
