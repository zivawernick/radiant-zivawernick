## Technical Challenges

THe biggest challange was creating a website that could effectively showcase Rabbi Wernick's extensive body of work and career experience, while also being user-friendly. 
I had to ensure that the design was clean and modern, while also reflecting the depth and wisdom of his teachings. And allowing for the ease of adding more content as he continues to publish new work and share sermons.
