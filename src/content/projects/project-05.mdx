## Project Overview

Transitioning from military to civilian life can be a daunting challenge. My Warrior's Shield provides veterans with the tools and community support they need to navigate this journey successfully, focusing on mental well-being and career development.

## My Role

As a Front-end Developer, I built the user interface for the resource hub and the community forum. I focused on accessibility and creating a calm, supportive visual environment for users who may be dealing with stress or trauma.

## How It Works

Veterans can access a curated library of resources, participate in moderated discussion forums, and use mental health tracking tools. The platform is designed to be a one-stop-shop for veteran support, bridging the gap between service and civilian life.
