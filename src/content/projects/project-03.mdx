## Project Overview

Homegrown Hero empowers local food systems by creating a direct link between producers and consumers. The platform simplifies the process of finding local farms, understanding their practices, and purchasing their goods, making sustainable eating accessible to everyone in the community.

## My Role

As a Front-end Developer and UX Designer, I focused on creating an engaging and user-friendly experience for both farmers and shoppers. I designed the map-based discovery tool and the streamlined checkout process, ensuring that the platform's mission was reflected in every interaction.

## How It Works

Farmers can create profiles to showcase their products and tell their story. Shoppers can search for specific items or browse local farms on an interactive map. The platform handles payments securely and facilitates communication between both parties, fostering a sense of community around local food.
