## Project Overview

At The Jaffa Institute, a non-profit combating poverty since 1982, I serve as Data Manager handling donor data, financial operations, website maintenance, and campaign execution. My work supports 40 programs aiding vulnerable populations in Tel Aviv-Jaffa with precise reporting and digital infrastructure.

## My Role

As Data Manager, I oversee the integrity and utility of our donor data, ensuring that we can effectively engage with our supporters and fund our vital programs. I also manage the organization's website and provide critical financial reporting to leadership.

## How It Works

We utilize a sophisticated CRM to track donor contributions and interactions, which informs our fundraising strategies and operational decisions. My role ensures that this data is accurate, accessible, and translated into actionable insights for the team.
The Jaffa Institute website uses Userway for accessibility and is built on Wix, allowing for easy updates and maintenance. I manage the content and structure of the site to ensure it effectively communicates our mission and impact. Our wix site includes pages for our various programs, impact reports, and ways to get involved, all designed to engage visitors and encourage support.
The website also includees custom-built backend functionality from all forms to link to our CRM and automate digital receipting for our American Friends of the Jaffa Institute donors. This has streamlined our operations and improved the donor experience.
