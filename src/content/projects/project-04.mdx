## Role Overview

At The Jaffa Institute, a non-profit combating poverty since 1982, I serve as Data Manager handling donor data, financial operations, website maintenance, and campaign execution. My work supports 40 programs aiding vulnerable populations in Tel Aviv-Jaffa with precise reporting and digital infrastructure.

## Key Responsibilities

I process 40+ American and 10+ Israeli donations monthly, issuing receipts within 2 business days, track bank transfers, and generate customized financial reports for leadership including net income analysis and currency conversions. Responsibilities include database hygiene like merging duplicates, grant data support, and security protocols.

I execute four major email campaigns yearly (Pesach, Summer, Rosh Hashanah, End-of-Year), segment lists, and ensure compliance with accessibility scans via Userway/Wix.

## Technical Accomplishments

Over five years, I started rebuilding the website in 2022 from the outdated 2021 version, launching the modern Wix platform in 2023 with custom code, database synchronization, and optimizations like automated receipting and payout reporting integration. I authored a complete Software Requirements Specification (SRS) for the donor database and manage its administration, access, and protocols.

Ongoing tasks include monthly reporting for stakeholders, privacy policy implementation with volunteers, and documentation for onboarding and operations continuity.

## Tech Stack
Wix (Custom Code & APIs), Donor CRM/Database, Citibank Reporting, JavaScript/HTML, Userway Accessibility, Google Drive/H Drive Archiving

## Impact

My contributions ensure transparent donor stewardship, accurate financial tracking, and seamless operations supporting the Institute's mission to end poverty cycles. This includes timely campaign execution driving revenue and robust data systems for grants and compliance.

**Ongoing Role: November 2020 – Present (5+ years).**
