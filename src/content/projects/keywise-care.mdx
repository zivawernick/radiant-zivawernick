## Project Overview

KeyWise Care provides boutique property management services for overseas homeowners in Israel. The website presents its service packages clearly, explains offerings like property inspections, bill payments, emergency coordination, and vehicle support, and builds trust through a clean and professional user experience.

## My Role

I built the site from scratch in Wix Studio, shaping both the visual direction and the content structure into a clear, professional client-facing experience. I organized the service offerings, created a maintainable page structure, and focused on delivering a polished site that could be updated easily over time.

I also supported the project with foundational marketing thinking, including clearer value propositions, lead-focused page flows, and a structure that could support future SEO and content updates.

## How It Works

The site presents three main service tiers with pricing and included services so visitors can quickly understand the level of support that fits their needs:
- **External Care** ($180/month): Visual exterior inspections, mail collection
- **Essential Care** ($260/month): Monthly interior checks, bill payments, water activation
- **Premium Care** ($390/month): Twice-monthly visits, emergency coordination, airport car service

Additional pages cover FAQs, services like car care and technician coordination, and build trust through professional design and clear value propositions.

## Technical Challenges

Because the project was built from scratch, one of the main challenges was defining the right structure, tone, and visual style while also moving quickly toward a working launch. Wix Studio made it possible to build responsive layouts, service-focused sections, and maintainable content in parallel without slowing down delivery.

Another challenge was balancing speed with long-term usability. I focused on building a site that looked polished at launch while also being simple enough to maintain, expand, and support future marketing efforts.

## Impact

The website gives KeyWise Care a professional online presence that clearly communicates its value to overseas homeowners in Israel. It makes the service easier to understand, supports trust with prospective clients, and creates a strong foundation for future growth.

The maintainable design means the client can update content without developer help, providing long-term value.
