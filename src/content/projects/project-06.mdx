## Project Overview

KeyWise Care provides boutique property management services for overseas homeowners in Israel. The website showcases their tiered service packages (External, Essential, Premium), clearly explains offerings like property inspections, bill payments, and emergency coordination, and builds trust through professional presentation.

[Live Site](https://www.keywisecare.com/)

## My Role

The main challenge was working with limited input from the founder while delivering a functional, professional site quickly. I focused on understanding the core service offering through the existing site content and built a clean, maintainable solution the client could update independently.

I chose Wix for rapid development with built-in CMS capabilities, ensuring the founder could easily manage service descriptions, pricing, and FAQs without technical expertise.

## How It Works

The site clearly presents three service tiers with pricing and features:
- **External Care** ($180/month): Visual exterior inspections, mail collection
- **Essential Care** ($260/month): Monthly interior checks, bill payments, water activation
- **Premium Care** ($390/month): Twice-monthly visits, emergency coordination, airport car service

Additional pages cover FAQs, services like car care and technician coordination, and build trust through professional design and clear value propositions.

## Technical Challenges

The biggest constraint was gathering detailed requirements from the busy founder. With limited time for strategy discussions, I prioritized building a solid MVP based on the existing service descriptions and common property management needs.

Wix's platform allowed rapid iteration: custom layouts for pricing tables, responsive design for international users, and built-in forms for inquiries. The CMS structure ensures the client can update pricing, add services, or modify FAQs independently.

## Impact

The website gives KeyWise Care professional online presence that clearly communicates their unique value to overseas property owners. It converts visitors into clients by making service packages transparent and trust-building.

The maintainable design means the founder can update content without developer help, providing long-term value.
