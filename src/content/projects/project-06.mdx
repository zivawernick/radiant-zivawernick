## Project Overview

Keywise Care is an on-demand car care service designed to make vehicle maintenance easy and accessible. Users can browse services, schedule appointments with qualified technicians, and track the progress of their car's care, all through an intuitive web platform.

## My Role

I led the front-end development of the platform, focusing on the service booking flow and the technician dashboard. I ensured that the application was responsive and user-friendly, providing a seamless experience for both car owners and service providers.

## How It Works

Users can select from a variety of car care services, choose a convenient time, and get connected with a local technician. The platform provides real-time updates and secure payment options, making it a reliable choice for vehicle maintenance.

Additional pages cover FAQs, services like car care and technician coordination, and build trust through professional design and clear value propositions.
